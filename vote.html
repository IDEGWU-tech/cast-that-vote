WELCOME TO CAST THAT VOTE

You can cast your vote from 8 am to 6 pm.

Citizen must be at least 18 years old with a national identification number.

Remember, it is one man one vote.

Voters Rights
Og Voters Rights

- Right to vote in free and fair elections.

- Right to be registered as a voter if eligible.

- Right to vote in secret and without intimidation.

- Right to access polling information and assistance.

- Right to demand accountability from elected leaders.
